Hi, this is Fan Xiao!
I’m a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Amsterdam (UvA). I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU). My research takes a broader feminist approach in examining grassroot communities and digital culture in Chinese speaking contexts. Focusing on artistic practices and popular gender discourses, my publications discuss issues such as labor and individual agency, transnational activist networks, and cultural resilience. Combining ethnographic approach with computational tools, my analyses delve in-depth into cultures and power relations of contemporary Chinese societies.
